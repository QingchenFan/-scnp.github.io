ABOUT LAB

The Laboratory of Social Cognitive Neuroscience in Psychiatry (SCNP), also known as the Brain-Mind-Society SCNP, was established in March 2022. It is the first research group with a joint principal investigator (PI) organizational structure in the Institute of Psychology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. The group is co-led by two researchers, Professor Gu Ruolei and Professor Zhou Yuan, who share similar research interests and complementary expertise. The SCNP focuses on the study of social cognitive deficits in individuals with psychological disorders, such as depression, and aims to investigate the underlying psychological mechanisms and biological basis of these deficits. The ultimate goal is to establish assessment criteria for social cognitive symptoms and develop intervention methods with practical applications.
